International

A few days after we published The Dvorak zine, DVzine.org was featured on the technology site Slashdot.org and tens of thousands of people from around the world read our zine. It was only when emails started pouring in from all over the world that we realized how America-centric our zine was! (Sadly, a common problem for Americans). Obviously, each language uses letters in different frequencies, and many of them have letters we don't even use!

While the American version of Dvorak might not work for all languages, the IDEAS behind the Dvorak Simplified Keyboard Layout can be applied to any keyboard design. On this page you'll find links to resources for other language variations of Dvorak.
